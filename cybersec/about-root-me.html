About Root-me.org

This post is mainly a rant about the rules on Root-me.org and how the website is managed.

As you may have noticed, my website hosts writeups about multiple Root-me challenges, which is against what's stated in their legal disclaimer. Now, why do I do that ? Well, mainly because I believe that people who are stuck deserve more than just cryptic and censored answers on a forum or shouldn't have to create a Discord account just to get a bit of help.

Now to those who will say that resources are linked on the challenges' pages, I find it hard to understand how a 1000 pages-long paywalled PDF file (yes, Yurichev now sell their book) is a good resource for a beginner who's stuck on a challenge. Also, I still don't understand how paywalling resources is a good idea, but hey, who am I to judge ?

Anyway, these writeups are there because I believe that repetition and adaptation of what's already been seen are key steps in learning, and that such informations should be freely accessible.

If you are on the Root-me team and want me to take these down, feel free to contact me via e-mail, my address is somewhere on this website.

Cheers
